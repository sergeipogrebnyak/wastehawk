Expense Approval Policy

Purpose: To establish clear guidelines for the approval of business-related expenses to ensure proper oversight and management of company funds.

Scope: This policy applies to all employees of WasteHawk.

Policy Statement:

  • All expenses exceeding $100 must be pre-approved by a department manager.
  • Employee reimbursements must be submitted with receipts and a completed Expense Reimbursement Form.
  • Expenses should be submitted within 30 days of the transaction.

Procedure:

  1. Employees request approval by submitting an Expense Request Form to their manager.
  2. Managers will review and either approve or deny the request, documenting the decision.
  3. Approved expenses will be reported to the finance team for processing.

Compliance: All employees are expected to comply with this policy. Violations may result in disciplinary action.
